Soongyu Choi (최순규)

Computer Architecture Researcher

Hi! I am Soongyu Choi(최순규), a Ph.D. student at KAIST under the supervision of Professor Joo-Young Kim. I previously earned my M.S. in Electrical Engineering from KAIST and my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Yonsei University.

My research covers a broad spectrum, ranging from NPU kernel optimization and domain-specific ASIC architecture to AI algorithm optimization and FPGA design. Recently, my focus has been on characterizing real-world multi-core NPUs and developing highly optimized kernels for them. My ultimate goal is to design an original ASIC architecture and bring it to market as a tangible commercial product.

Research Interests

  • AI Accelerators
  • Memory Systems
  • Processing-in-Memory
  • System Co-Design
  • Energy-Efficient Computing
